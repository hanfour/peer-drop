PeerDrop comes to Mac.

- Bidirectional voice with iPhone: tap the phone icon on either device; the other side shows the incoming call within a second.
- Drag files from Finder onto the Dock icon, the main window, or a peer's row in the menu bar.
- Multi-window chat — one window per peer; ⌘1 jumps to the latest.
- Menu bar item with status, peer list, and Pet sprite.
- Settings live in the standard System Settings location (⌘,) — General, Profile, Relay.

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. Sandboxed; no full disk access, no analytics, no accounts.

Compatibility: the Mac app talks to iPhone/iPad v5.0+ peers unchanged.
