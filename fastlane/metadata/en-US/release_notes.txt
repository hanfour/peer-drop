PeerDrop 3.2.2

Fixes
- Definitively resolved the relay -1011 WebSocket error. Reconnects now dedup by client ID, so retries evict their own stale socket instead of piling up and triggering "room is full".
- QR code and deep link now include the device's IP even when it is sharing a Personal Hotspot (bridge interface), so scanners can reach it over the hotspot without falling back to relay.
