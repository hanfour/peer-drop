Species-Specific Behaviors
- Each pet species has unique physics and behavior patterns
- Cats scratch and stretch, dogs dig and wag, rabbits hop and burrow
- Birds and dragons fly, ghosts float through walls, slimes bounce and melt
- Pets spontaneously leave and return with species-specific animations
- Pets interact with chat messages (cats jump on bubbles, dogs sniff, ghosts pass through)

Relay Diagnostics
- Detailed error reporting at every relay connection failure point
- "Send Error Report" button in connection error alerts
- Error reports include full diagnostic context for remote debugging
