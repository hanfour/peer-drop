PeerDrop 5.0 — Pets Come Alive

What's new:
- Pets now walk with full multi-frame animations across every direction.
- Subtle idle breathing brings pets to life when they're at rest.
- Direction changes are smoother — your pet keeps animating mid-turn.

Behind the scenes:
- New sprite pipeline supports per-action frame arrays (walk, idle).
- Bundle size is slightly larger to accommodate the new frames.
- All v4 features and your saved pet are unchanged.
