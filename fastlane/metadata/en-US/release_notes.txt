PeerDrop 5.0.1 — Quick follow-up to 5.0

What's new:
- Retired the Ghost species. We were never satisfied with the way it looked, and rather than ship more partial fixes, we removed it entirely.

If your pet was a Ghost:
- Don't worry — your pet is preserved. Same name, same age, same level, same memories. Only the species visual changes (now a Cat). Nothing is lost.

Behind the scenes:
- Smaller bundle, less to download.
- Migration runs silently on first launch. No action needed from you.
