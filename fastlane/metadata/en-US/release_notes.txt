PeerDrop 2.1.0 — Pet Evolution Overhaul

New Pet System
- 10 pet body types: Cat, Dog, Rabbit, Bird, Frog, Bear, Dragon, Octopus, Ghost, Slime
- Baby and Child evolution stages with 20 animated actions each
- 16×16 pixel art sprites with 8 color palettes

Physical Interactions
- Pets walk, climb walls, and hang from the ceiling
- Drag and throw your pet with gravity and bounce physics
- Pet stroke gesture for hearts and XP

Food System
- Item inventory: Rice Ball, Fish, Apple
- Drag food onto screen — your pet chases and eats it!
- Daily login food refresh, earn food from peer connections

Evolution & Growth
- Accumulate XP + time to evolve to the next stage
- Haptic feedback and flash effects on evolution
- Name your pet when it hatches

Dynamic Island & Widgets
- Live Activity on Dynamic Island shows pet status
- Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets
