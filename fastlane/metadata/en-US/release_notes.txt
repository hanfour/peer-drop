PeerDrop 3.3.1 — Relay Reliability Hotfix

Fixes
- Relay connections occasionally failed within seconds with a WebSocket error (-1011). Previously, any failed relay handshake left a socket lingering on the server, and two such leftovers would make the room reject all new joiners. Every failure path now properly closes its signaling connection, and the server evicts stale sockets before refusing a new peer.
- Restored the 30-second relay handshake timeout. 20 seconds was too aggressive for TURN-over-TLS on cellular or high-latency networks and caused premature failures.

Also includes the full v3.3 cross-network experience from 3.3.0.
