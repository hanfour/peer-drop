PeerDrop 5.0.2 — Pets actually animate now

The walking animation that shipped with 5.0 was silently broken — pets moved across the screen but their walk sprite never cycled, so it looked like they were sliding instead of walking. Fixed.

What's new:
- Cats now properly cycle through their 8-frame walking animation (was stuck on frame 0).
- Pets bounce off side walls instead of pressing into them.
- Pets walk along the floor above the tab bar (no more drifting in the middle of the screen).
- Dragging-and-releasing a pet drops it cleanly. No more unintended little hops on landing.
- Evolution progress now shows your pet's age (the actual measure that matters), not a confusing XP bar.

The cat is currently the only species with full multi-frame walking. Other species still render correctly but as single-frame static sprites — more species get walking animations in future updates.

If your pet still looks stuck:
- Force-quit and reopen the app. The fix lands on next launch.
