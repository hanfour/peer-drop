PeerDrop 5.0 — Pets Come Alive

What's new:
- Pets gained multi-frame walk animations and subtle idle breathing — they actually move now instead of sliding.
- Smoother direction changes: animation continues mid-turn instead of resetting.
- Initial coverage focuses on common species; more pets gain animations in future updates.

Bug fixes:
- Fixed a long-standing bug where pets aged into "elder" too quickly. Pets now stay adult longer; elder is reserved for genuinely long-lived pets with active engagement.
- Ghost pet redesigned with a properly ghostly look (translucent wisp form, no longer Pac-Man-shaped).

Behind the scenes:
- New sprite pipeline supports per-action frame arrays.
- All v4 features and your saved pet are preserved.
