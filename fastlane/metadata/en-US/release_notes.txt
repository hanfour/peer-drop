PeerDrop 3.3.0 — Cross-Network Experience

New
- Smart suggestions adapt to your environment: invite a known device, use your tailnet, create a relay room, or try Tailscale when things keep failing.
- Tailnet peer management: add Tailscale peers once and PeerDrop probes them every 60s so they appear in Nearby automatically.
- TURN-over-TLS (port 5349) for corporate and restrictive networks where UDP is blocked.

Improvements
- Faster relay handshake with candidate pool pre-warming and phase-aware timeouts (8s direct → 20s give up).
- Adaptive ICE policy: networks that repeatedly require relay rescue now skip direct attempts automatically.
- Anonymous connection telemetry to help us ship reliable cross-network connects (no personal data, opt-out via remote config).
