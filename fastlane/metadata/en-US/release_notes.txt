What's new in PeerDrop 1.2.0:

- Relay enabled by default — share across different networks out of the box
- Clipboard sync — automatically share clipboard content with connected peers
- Message edit & delete — long press to edit or delete sent messages within 5 minutes
- File transfer resume — reconnect and continue interrupted transfers
- Nearby Interaction — see distance and direction to connected devices
- Connection list improvements — transfer speed, connection duration display
